# Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Looplly.

## Getting Started

### How do I create an account?

1. Visit looplly.com and click "Sign Up"
2. Enter your email, name, and mobile number
3. Verify your mobile via SMS
4. Complete your profile to start earning

### Is Looplly really free?

Yes! There are no fees to join or use Looplly. We pay you for your time and opinions.

### What age do I need to be?

Minimum age varies by country:
- South Africa: 18 years old
- Most countries: 16-18 years old

### Do I need to download an app?

No! Looplly works in any web browser. However, we do have mobile apps available for iOS and Android for a better experience.

## Earning & Rewards

### How much can I earn?

Earnings vary based on:
- Survey length and complexity
- Your profile completeness
- Your reputation tier
- Referral activity
- Daily streak bonuses

Most active users earn R200-R500 per month. Top earners can make R1000+.

### When do I get paid?

- Survey rewards: Instant
- Referral bonuses: When conditions are met
- Cash-out requests: 3-5 business days

### What's the minimum cash-out amount?

- Bank transfer: R100
- Mobile money: R50
- Gift cards: R25

### Why don't I qualify for surveys?

Common reasons:
- Incomplete profile
- Quota full (survey has enough responses)
- Demographics don't match survey requirements
- Previous quality issues

**Tip:** Complete your Level 2 profile to qualify for more opportunities.

### What happens if my survey is rejected?

Rejected surveys don't earn rewards and may affect your reputation score. Common causes:
- Speeding through questions
- Inconsistent answers
- Not reading questions carefully
- Straight-lining (same answer for all questions)

## Profile & Account

### How do I update my profile?

1. Go to **Profile** tab in your dashboard
2. Click on any section to edit
3. Update information
4. Click "Save"

### Can I change my mobile number?

Yes, but you'll need to verify the new number. Contact support if you need help.

### How do I delete my account?

Go to **Settings** → **Account** → "Delete Account". Note: This is permanent and cannot be undone.

### Is my data secure?

Yes! We use bank-level encryption and never sell your personal information. You control what you share.

## Reputation & Streaks

### What is reputation?

Reputation is your trust score on Looplly. It's based on:
- Survey completion rate
- Response quality
- Activity consistency
- Profile accuracy

Higher reputation = better opportunities and bonuses.

### How do I improve my reputation?

- Complete surveys honestly and thoroughly
- Maintain daily check-in streaks
- Keep your profile updated
- Refer active friends
- Participate in community

### What happens if I miss a day in my streak?

Your streak resets to 0. Start building again from day 1!

### Can reputation go down?

Yes. Reputation decreases from:
- Rejected surveys
- Abandoned surveys
- Inactivity (profile goes stale)
- Quality issues

## Referrals

### How does the referral program work?

1. Share your unique referral link
2. Friends sign up using your link
3. You earn when they complete their first survey
4. Get ongoing bonuses for active referrals

[Learn more about Referrals](referral-program.md)

### When do I get my referral bonus?

- Sign-up bonus: When friend verifies mobile
- Activity bonus: When friend completes first survey
- Monthly bonus: For each active referral

### Can I refer family members?

Yes! But each person needs their own device and email. Account sharing is not allowed.

## Technical Issues

### I didn't receive my verification code

Try these steps:
1. Check if you entered the correct number
2. Wait 2-3 minutes for delivery
3. Check spam/junk folder (for email codes)
4. Request a new code
5. Contact support if problem persists

### My survey won't load

- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser
- Contact support with details

### I completed a survey but didn't get paid

- Check your transaction history in the Wallet tab
- Sometimes processing takes a few minutes
- If still missing after 15 minutes, contact support with survey details

## Privacy & Security

### What information do you collect?

We collect:
- Profile information you provide
- Survey responses
- Activity on the platform
- Device and browser information (for security)

[Read our full Privacy Policy](privacy-policy.md)

### Do you share my information?

We share only anonymized, aggregated data with research partners. Your personal information is never sold or shared with third parties without your consent.

### How do I control my privacy settings?

Go to **Settings** → **Privacy** to manage:
- Communication preferences
- Data sharing settings
- Marketing opt-ins
- Location services
