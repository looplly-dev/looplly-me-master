# How to Earn on Looplly

There are multiple ways to earn rewards on Looplly. This guide covers all earning opportunities available to you.

## Ways to Earn

### 1. Complete Surveys

Surveys are the primary way to earn on Looplly.

**How it works:**
- Check the **Earn** tab for available surveys
- Each survey shows estimated time and reward amount
- Complete the survey honestly and thoroughly
- Rewards are credited immediately upon completion

**Typical Earnings:**
- Quick surveys (5-10 min): R5 - R15
- Standard surveys (10-20 min): R15 - R35
- In-depth surveys (20-30 min): R35 - R75

**Tips for Success:**
- Answer honestly - quality matters more than speed
- Complete your profile for better matches
- Check daily for new opportunities
- Maintain a good reputation score

### 2. Profile Completion

Earn rewards by building and maintaining your profile.

**Earnings:**
- Complete Level 1 profile: R10 bonus
- Complete Level 2 profile: R25 bonus
- Update stale profile information: R5 per update
- Answer new profile questions: R2-5 each

### 3. Refer Friends

Earn when your friends join and participate.

**How it works:**
- Share your unique referral link
- Friend signs up using your link
- You earn when they complete their first survey
- Bonus for friends who stay active

**Earnings:**
- Friend joins: R5
- Friend completes first survey: R15
- Active referral bonus: R5/month per active referral

[Learn more about the Referral Program](referral-program.md)

### 4. Daily Check-ins

Build your streak by checking in daily.

**How it works:**
- Visit the **Rep** tab daily
- Click "Check In" to mark your streak
- Longer streaks = better rewards
- Miss a day and your streak resets

**Earnings:**
- 7-day streak: R10 bonus
- 30-day streak: R50 bonus
- 90-day streak: R200 bonus + badge

### 5. Community Participation

Engage with the Looplly community.

**How it works:**
- Share helpful content
- Help other members
- Participate in discussions
- Complete community challenges

**Earnings:**
- Varies by activity
- Typically R2-10 per contribution

## Reputation & Earnings

Your reputation affects your earning potential:

- **Bronze Tier** - Standard survey rates
- **Silver Tier** - 10% bonus on survey rewards
- **Gold Tier** - 20% bonus + exclusive surveys
- **Diamond Tier** - 30% bonus + VIP opportunities

[Learn more about the Reputation System](reputation-system.md)

## Payment & Wallet

All earnings are tracked in your **Wallet**.

**Wallet Features:**
- Real-time balance tracking
- Transaction history
- Pending vs. available balance
- Cash-out options

**Minimum Cash-out:**
- Bank transfer: R100 minimum
- Mobile money: R50 minimum
- Gift cards: R25 minimum

[Learn more about Cash-out Methods](../help/cashout-methods.md)

## Earning Tips

1. **Complete your profile fully** - Unlocks more opportunities
2. **Check daily** - New surveys are added regularly
3. **Be honest** - Quality responses build reputation
4. **Build your streak** - Daily check-ins add up
5. **Refer friends** - Passive income from active referrals
6. **Stay active** - Regular participation = better opportunities

## Earning Limits

To ensure quality and prevent abuse:

- **Stage 1 Users** (unverified): R50/month cap
- **Stage 2 Users** (verified, Level 1): R200/month cap
- **Stage 3 Users** (verified, Level 2): Unlimited earning

*Stage 3 unlocks after mobile verification + Level 2 profile completion*

## Common Questions

**Q: When do I get paid?**
A: Survey rewards are credited immediately. Cash-outs process within 3-5 business days.

**Q: Why was my survey rejected?**
A: Surveys may be rejected for incomplete answers, speeding through, or inconsistent responses. This affects your reputation score.

**Q: How do I qualify for more surveys?**
A: Complete your profile, maintain a good reputation, and check regularly for new opportunities.
