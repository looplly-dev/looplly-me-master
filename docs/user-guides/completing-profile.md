# How to Complete Your Profile

A complete profile helps us match you with the best earning opportunities and ensures you get the most relevant surveys.

## Why Complete Your Profile?

- **Better Matches** - Get surveys that are relevant to you
- **Higher Earnings** - Access more opportunities
- **Build Reputation** - Complete profiles earn trust and better rates
- **Unlock Features** - Some activities require a verified profile

## Profile Levels

Your profile has two levels of completion:

### Level 1: Basic Information

These are the essentials we need to get you started:

- **Name** - Your first and last name
- **Mobile Number** - For verification and notifications
- **Email** - For account recovery and updates
- **Date of Birth** - To verify age requirements
- **Country** - To match you with local opportunities

**Time to Complete:** 2-3 minutes

### Level 2: Detailed Profile

This information helps us match you with better opportunities:

- **Gender** - For demographic matching
- **Address** - For location-based surveys
- **Income Range** - Household and personal income
- **Ethnicity** - For inclusive research
- **Socio-Economic Classification (SEC)** - For market research

**Time to Complete:** 5-7 minutes

## Step-by-Step Guide

### 1. Navigate to Your Profile

From your dashboard, click on the **Profile** tab in the bottom navigation.

### 2. Complete Level 1

Fill in the basic fields marked with an asterisk (*). These are required to start earning.

### 3. Verify Your Mobile

- Enter your mobile number with country code
- Receive verification code via SMS
- Enter the code to verify

### 4. Complete Level 2 (Optional but Recommended)

Once Level 1 is complete, you can add more details to unlock better opportunities:

- Fill in additional demographic information
- Enable location services if comfortable
- Answer optional preference questions

### 5. Keep Your Profile Updated

We'll occasionally ask you to update your profile to ensure accuracy. You'll earn reputation points for keeping information current!

## Privacy & Control

- You control what information you share
- You can update or remove information anytime
- Your data is encrypted and secure
- We never sell your personal information

## Profile Completeness Score

Your profile shows a completeness percentage:

- **0-40%** - Basic setup only
- **41-70%** - Good progress, more opportunities available
- **71-90%** - Great profile, most opportunities unlocked
- **91-100%** - Complete profile, maximum earning potential
