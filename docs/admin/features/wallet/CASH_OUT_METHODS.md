## Mobile Airtime üí≥

### Overview
Convert your earnings directly to mobile airtime/credit on your phone. Perfect for frequent, small withdrawals.

### Key Details
- **Minimum Withdrawal**: $1.00
- **Maximum**: No limit (but subject to your Available Balance)
- **Processing Time**: Instant to 24 hours
- **Fees**: No platform fees (carrier may have limits)
- **Availability**: Most countries supported

### Supported Carriers
- **Africa**: MTN, Vodacom, Safaricom, Airtel, Telkom, Cell C
- **Asia**: Globe, Smart, Sun Cellular, and more
- **Other**: Check availability for your country

### How It Works

1. **Select Mobile Airtime**
   - Click "Cash Out" in your wallet
   - Choose "Mobile Airtime" option
   - Minimum $1.00 required in Available Balance

2. **Enter Your Mobile Number**
   - Use the same number registered to your account
   - Format: International format (e.g., +27821234567)
   - Double-check the number (errors cannot be reversed)

3. **Enter Amount**
   - Must be at least $1.00
   - Cannot exceed your Available Balance
   - Amount shown in USD, converted to local airtime value

4. **Confirm Transaction**
   - Review mobile number and amount
   - Click "Confirm Cash Out"
   - Transaction submitted for AI verification

5. **Receive Airtime**
   - Verification: 2-4 hours (usually faster)
   - Delivery: Instant to 24 hours after approval
   - Check your mobile balance to confirm receipt

### Pros & Cons

**Advantages** ‚úÖ
- Lowest minimum ($1.00)
- Fast processing
- No platform fees
- Widely available
- No bank account needed

**Limitations** ‚ö†Ô∏è
- Can only use on your registered mobile number
- Airtime has no cash value (can't convert back)
- Some carriers have daily top-up limits
- Exchange rate from USD may vary

### Troubleshooting

**Issue**: "Airtime not received after 24 hours"
- **Check**: Transaction status in wallet history
- **Verify**: Mobile number was entered correctly
- **Contact**: Your mobile carrier to check if credit is pending
- **Action**: Contact Looplly support with transaction ID

**Issue**: "Invalid mobile number error"
- **Check**: Number format includes country code (e.g., +27)
- **Verify**: Number matches your registered profile
- **Fix**: Update mobile number in Settings if needed

## M-Pesa üì±

### Overview
Mobile money transfer popular in East Africa. Withdraw directly to your M-Pesa mobile wallet for instant access to cash.

### Key Details
- **Minimum Withdrawal**: $5.00
- **Maximum**: Varies by country (typically $500 per transaction)
- **Processing Time**: Instant to 24 hours
- **Fees**: Small M-Pesa transaction fee (varies by amount)
- **Availability**: Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, Mozambique, Ghana, Egypt, Lesotho, DRC

### Supported Countries & Operators

#### **Kenya** üá∞üá™
- Operator: Safaricom M-Pesa
- Daily limit: KES 150,000 (~$1,150)
- Most widely used

#### **Tanzania** üáπüáø
- Operators: Vodacom M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money
- Daily limit: TZS 500,000 (~$210)

#### **Uganda** üá∫üá¨
- Operators: MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money
- Daily limit: UGX 2,000,000 (~$530)

#### **Other Countries**
- Mozambique, Ghana, Egypt, Lesotho, DRC
- Limits vary by country and operator

### How It Works

1. **Select M-Pesa**
   - Click "Cash Out" in your wallet
   - Choose "M-Pesa" option
   - Minimum $5.00 required

2. **Enter M-Pesa Number**
   - Use your registered M-Pesa mobile number
   - Format: +254XXXXXXXXX (Kenya) or local format
   - Must be the same number on your Looplly account

3. **Enter Amount**
   - Minimum: $5.00
   - Maximum: Your Available Balance (or country limit)
   - Amount displayed in USD, credited in local currency

4. **Review Exchange Rate**
   - Current USD to local currency rate shown
   - M-Pesa fee displayed (if applicable)
   - Final amount you'll receive in local currency

5. **Confirm & Receive**
   - Confirm transaction details
   - AI verification: 2-4 hours
   - M-Pesa transfer: Instant to 24 hours
   - You'll receive M-Pesa SMS confirmation

### Fees & Exchange Rates

**Platform Fees**: None from Looplly

**M-Pesa Fees** (charged by M-Pesa, not Looplly):
- Kenya: ~1-2% of transaction value
- Tanzania: ~1.5-3%
- Uganda: ~1-2.5%
- Exact fee shown before confirmation

**Exchange Rate**:
- Live market rate used
- Updated hourly
- Displayed before you confirm

**Example Transaction** (Kenya):
```
Withdrawal Amount: $10.00
Exchange Rate: 1 USD = 135 KES
Gross Amount: KES 1,350
M-Pesa Fee: KES 27 (2%)
You Receive: KES 1,323
```

### Pros & Cons

**Advantages** ‚úÖ
- Fast processing (often instant after approval)
- Withdraw to mobile wallet (no bank needed)
- Can easily convert to cash at agents
- Widely accepted for payments
- Good minimum balance ($5)

**Limitations** ‚ö†Ô∏è
- Only available in select countries
- M-Pesa charges transaction fees
- Daily limits apply
- Must have registered M-Pesa account
- Exchange rate fluctuates

### Troubleshooting

**Issue**: "M-Pesa number not recognized"
- **Check**: Number includes correct country code
- **Verify**: M-Pesa account is active and registered
- **Fix**: Ensure number matches your Looplly profile

**Issue**: "Transaction failed - exceeds limit"
- **Reason**: Exceeded daily M-Pesa limit
- **Solution**: Wait 24 hours or withdraw smaller amount
- **Alternative**: Use Crypto for larger amounts

**Issue**: "Money not received in M-Pesa wallet"
- **Check**: Transaction status shows "Completed"
- **Wait**: Up to 24 hours for M-Pesa processing
- **Verify**: M-Pesa SMS confirmation
- **Action**: Contact M-Pesa customer service with transaction ID

## Cryptocurrency ‚Çø

### Overview
Withdraw earnings to your crypto wallet. Best for larger amounts and users comfortable with blockchain technology.

### Key Details
- **Minimum Withdrawal**: $10.00
- **Maximum**: No limit (subject to Available Balance)
- **Processing Time**: 24-48 hours
- **Fees**: Network transaction fees (typically $0.50-$2.00)
- **Availability**: Global

### Supported Cryptocurrencies

#### **Bitcoin (BTC)** ‚Çø
- Most popular option
- Highest security
- Network fee: ~$1-2 (varies by network congestion)
- Confirmation time: 10-60 minutes after sending

#### **Ethereum (ETH)** Œû
- Fast transactions
- Widely supported
- Network fee: ~$0.50-$3 (varies by gas price)
- Confirmation time: 1-5 minutes after sending

#### **Tether (USDT)** ‚ÇÆ
- Stablecoin (pegged to USD)
- No exchange rate volatility
- Available on multiple networks (Ethereum, Tron, Polygon)
- Network fee varies by network

#### **Coming Soon**
- Litecoin (LTC)
- Bitcoin Cash (BCH)
- USD Coin (USDC)

### How It Works

1. **Select Cryptocurrency**
   - Click "Cash Out" in wallet
   - Choose "Cryptocurrency" option
   - Minimum $10.00 required

2. **Choose Your Crypto**
   - Select Bitcoin, Ethereum, or USDT
   - If USDT, choose network (Ethereum, Tron, etc.)
   - Each has different fee structures

3. **Enter Wallet Address**
   - Paste your crypto wallet address
   - **CRITICAL**: Triple-check address (typos = lost funds)
   - Use QR scan if available
   - Ensure address matches selected cryptocurrency

4. **Enter Amount**
   - Minimum: $10.00
   - Maximum: Available Balance
   - Amount shown in USD and crypto equivalent

5. **Review & Confirm**
   - Verify wallet address one more time
   - Check network fee estimate
   - Review final amount you'll receive
   - Confirm transaction

6. **AI Verification**
   - Transaction submitted to AI Accountant
   - Verification: 4-12 hours
   - Security checks for fraud prevention

7. **Blockchain Processing**
   - Once approved, sent to blockchain
   - Transaction hash provided for tracking
   - Confirmations: 10 minutes - 2 hours
   - Check your wallet for arrival

### Fees & Processing

**Network Fees** (paid to blockchain, not Looplly):
- **Bitcoin**: $1-2 (higher during network congestion)
- **Ethereum**: $0.50-$3 (depends on gas price)
- **USDT (Tron)**: $0.10-$1 (cheapest option)

**Total Processing Time**:
1. AI Verification: 4-12 hours
2. Blockchain broadcast: Immediate after approval
3. Network confirmations: 10 minutes - 2 hours
4. Total: 24-48 hours maximum

**Example Transaction**:
```
Withdrawal Amount: $50.00
Cryptocurrency: USDT (Tron)
Network Fee: $1.00
You Receive: $49.00 USDT
Processing: 24-36 hours
```

### Pros & Cons

**Advantages** ‚úÖ
- Global availability
- No daily limits
- Decentralized (you control funds)
- Can hold as investment
- Relatively low fees for large amounts

**Limitations** ‚ö†Ô∏è
- Requires crypto wallet and knowledge
- Higher minimum ($10)
- Longer processing time (24-48 hrs)
- Network fees apply
- Exchange rate volatility (except USDT)
- Irreversible (wrong address = lost funds)

### Security & Best Practices

‚úÖ **Always**:
- Triple-check wallet address before confirming
- Use QR code scanner to avoid typos
- Verify you're using correct network (e.g., Ethereum vs Tron for USDT)
- Save transaction hash for tracking
- Use reputable wallet providers

‚ùå **Never**:
- Share your private keys or seed phrase
- Send to exchange deposit address (may have tag/memo requirements)
- Use unverified or unknown wallet services
- Assume transaction is instant (wait for confirmations)

### Recommended Wallets

**Mobile Wallets**:
- Trust Wallet (multi-coin)
- Coinbase Wallet
- MetaMask (Ethereum/USDT)
- Exodus (multi-coin)

**Hardware Wallets** (most secure):
- Ledger Nano
- Trezor

**Exchange Wallets** (use with caution):
- Binance
- Coinbase
- Kraken
- Note: Check if they require memo/tag for deposits

### Troubleshooting

**Issue**: "Invalid wallet address"
- **Check**: Address matches selected cryptocurrency
- **Verify**: Using correct network (Ethereum vs Tron for USDT)
- **Fix**: Copy address directly from your wallet (don't type manually)

**Issue**: "Transaction shows completed but crypto not received"
- **Check**: Blockchain explorer using transaction hash
- **Verify**: Confirmations are complete (10-60 minutes)
- **Wait**: Some wallets need multiple confirmations to display
- **Action**: If 2+ hours, contact support with transaction hash

**Issue**: "Sent to wrong address"
- **Reality**: Cryptocurrency transactions are irreversible
- **Prevention**: Always triple-check address before confirming
- **Note**: Looplly cannot recover funds sent to wrong address

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I use multiple withdrawal methods?

**Yes!** You can use different methods for different withdrawals. Choose based on:
- Amount available
- Urgency needed
- Fees involved
- Regional availability

---

### How long does verification take?

**AI Accountant Verification**:
- Most transactions: 2-4 hours
- High-value transactions: 4-12 hours
- First withdrawal: May take 24 hours (extra security)

**After Verification**:
- Mobile Airtime: Instant - 24 hours
- M-Pesa: Instant - 24 hours
- Crypto: Immediate broadcast, 10 mins - 2 hours for confirmations

---

### Why can't I withdraw my pending balance?

Only **Available Balance** can be withdrawn. Pending balance needs AI verification first (24-48 hours). This protects against:
- Fraudulent earnings
- Provider payment failures
- Duplicate transactions
- Account security issues

---

### Can I cancel a withdrawal?

**Before AI Approval**: Contact support immediately (may be possible)

**After AI Approval**: Cannot be cancelled
- Mobile Airtime: Irreversible once sent
- M-Pesa: Irreversible once sent
- Crypto: Blockchain transactions cannot be reversed

**Best Practice**: Always double-check details before confirming.

---

### What if I entered wrong details?

**Wrong Mobile Number/M-Pesa**:
- If funds sent, you cannot recover them
- Contact support immediately (may help if not yet processed)
- Prevention: Always verify number before confirming

**Wrong Crypto Address**:
- Blockchain transactions are irreversible
- Funds sent to wrong address are lost forever
- Prevention: Triple-check address, use QR scan

---

### Are there withdrawal limits?

**Platform Limits**:
- No maximum from Looplly side
- Limited only by your Available Balance

**Method Limits**:
- Mobile Airtime: Carrier limits may apply
- M-Pesa: Country-specific daily limits ($200-$500)
- Crypto: No limits
- PayPal: TBD (when available)
