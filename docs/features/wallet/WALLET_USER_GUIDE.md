# Wallet User Guide

## Overview

Your Looplly wallet is the central hub for managing all your earnings. This guide covers everything you need to know about viewing balances, understanding transaction history, and withdrawing your hard-earned money.

## Understanding Your Balance

### Balance Types

Your wallet displays two key balance types:

#### **Available Balance**
- Money you can withdraw **right now**
- Already verified and cleared by our AI Accountant
- Ready for cash-out to your preferred method
- Displayed prominently at the top of your wallet

#### **Pending Balance**
- Money currently being verified
- Recent earnings that need AI verification (typically 24-48 hours)
- Includes transactions marked as "Verifying" or "Awaiting Funds"
- Automatically moves to Available Balance once verified

**Example:**
```
Available Balance: $15.35 ‚Üê Withdraw anytime
Pending Balance: $3.20 ‚Üê Being verified
Total Earned: $18.55
```

## Transaction History

### Understanding Transaction Status

Each transaction in your wallet has a status indicator:

| Status | Meaning | Action Required |
|--------|---------|-----------------|
| **Completed** | Payment received and verified | None - money in Available Balance |
| **Verifying** | AI Accountant checking transaction | Wait 24-48 hours |
| **Awaiting Funds** | Provider processing payment | Wait for provider confirmation |
| **Pending** | Recently submitted | System processing |
| **Failed** | Transaction could not be completed | Contact support if needed |

### Transaction Details

Each transaction shows:
- **Amount**: How much you earned (in USD)
- **Type**: Source of earning (survey, video, task, referral, etc.)
- **Date**: When the transaction was created
- **Status**: Current verification state
- **Description**: Brief details about the earning activity

## How to Withdraw Funds

### Step-by-Step Cash-Out Process

1. **Check Your Available Balance**
   - Ensure you meet the minimum for your chosen method
   - Only Available Balance can be withdrawn (not Pending)

2. **Click "Cash Out"**
   - Located at the top of your Wallet tab
   - Opens the cash-out method selector

3. **Choose Your Method**
   - Select from Mobile Airtime, M-Pesa, or Crypto
   - Each shows minimum amount and processing time

4. **Enter Details**
   - **Mobile Airtime/M-Pesa**: Enter your mobile number
   - **Crypto**: Enter your wallet address and select coin
   - Double-check all details before confirming

5. **Confirm Transaction**
   - Review amount and destination
   - Click "Confirm Cash Out"
   - Transaction submitted to AI Accountant

6. **Wait for Verification**
   - AI Accountant reviews for fraud prevention
   - Typically approved within 2-4 hours
   - You'll see status updates in transaction history

7. **Receive Your Money**
   - Once approved, funds sent to your chosen method
   - Mobile Airtime/M-Pesa: Usually within 1 hour
   - Crypto: 24-48 hours for blockchain confirmation

## AI Accountant System

### What is The Accountant?

The Accountant is our AI-powered transaction verification system that:
- ‚úÖ Verifies all incoming earnings are legitimate
- ‚úÖ Detects and prevents fraudulent activity
- ‚úÖ Approves cash-out requests after security checks
- ‚úÖ Protects your account from unauthorized transactions

### Why Verification Takes Time

**Incoming Earnings (24-48 hours):**
- Confirms survey/task completion with providers
- Validates reward amounts match activity
- Checks for duplicate or fraudulent submissions
- Ensures funds are actually received from partners

**Outgoing Withdrawals (2-4 hours):**
- Verifies account ownership
- Checks withdrawal destination matches your profile
- Confirms sufficient Available Balance
- Prevents unauthorized access attempts

**Note**: Verification times are maximum estimates. Most transactions clear much faster.

## Common Issues & Solutions

### "Why is my balance pending?"

**Reason**: All new earnings require AI verification for fraud prevention.

**Timeline**: 24-48 hours for most transactions.

**Action**: No action needed. Check back in 24 hours.

---

### "Cash Out button is disabled"

**Possible Reasons**:
1. ‚ùå Available Balance below minimum ($1 for Airtime, $5 for M-Pesa, $10 for Crypto)
2. ‚ùå Profile incomplete (need verified mobile number)
3. ‚ùå Account verification pending

**Solution**: 
- Check your Available Balance amount
- Complete profile verification
- Wait for pending verifications to clear

---

### "Transaction shows as Failed"

**Common Causes**:
- Incorrect mobile number or wallet address
- Provider service temporarily down
- Insufficient balance (attempted before verification)
- Duplicate transaction detected

**Action**: 
1. Check transaction details for error message
2. Retry with correct information
3. If problem persists, contact support with transaction ID

---

### "Pending balance not moving to Available"

**Typical Reasons**:
- Still within 24-48 hour verification window
- Provider hasn't confirmed payment yet
- Transaction flagged for manual review (rare)

**Action**: 
- Wait full 48 hours before contacting support
- Check transaction status in history
- Ensure the earning activity was completed properly

## Security & Privacy

‚úÖ **We Do**:

- Use AI-powered fraud detection
- Require verification for withdrawals
- Monitor for suspicious activity

‚úÖ **You Should**:
