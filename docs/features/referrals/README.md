## Common Questions

### How much can I earn from referrals?

**Realistic Ranges**:
- **Casual** (1-2 referrals/month): $5-10/year
- **Active** (7-10 referrals/month): $30-40/year
- **Super** (15-20 referrals/month): $60-80/year
- **Influencer** (30+ referrals/month): $125+/year

**Plus**: Stack with survey/video earnings for total income.

---

### What if my friend doesn't qualify?

**Normal**: 30-50% won't qualify (typical).

**Why**:
- Lost interest
- Too busy
- Unrealistic expectations
- Never started earning

**What To Do**:
- Check in after 2-3 days
- Offer help
- Don't be pushy
- Focus on volume + support

---

### How long until I get paid?

**Timeline**:
1. Friend earns $0.35: Triggers qualification
2. AI verification: 24-48 hours
3. Your bonus: Added to Pending Balance
4. Clears to Available: 24-48 hours
5. Can withdraw: Immediately

**Total**: 3-5 days from qualification
