### "What if my friend doesn't qualify?"

**Normal**: 30-50% of signups never qualify (typical drop-off).

**Reasons**:
- Lost interest
- Too busy
- Didn't understand how to earn
- Unrealistic expectations
- Technical issues

**What You Can Do**:
- Follow up after 2-3 days
- Offer help getting started
- Share tips for earning quickly
- But don't be pushy (some people just won't engage)

**Accept**: Not everyone will qualify, focus on volume + support.

### "Can I refer myself or family members?"

**Same Household**:
‚úÖ **Allowed**: Refer family members (spouse, siblings, etc.)
‚ùå **Not Allowed**: Multiple accounts for same person

**Requirements**:
- Each person must have own device
- Own email and mobile number
- Own earning activity (no shared devices)

**System Detects**: Multiple accounts from same device/IP
- Will flag for review
- May invalidate referral bonus
- Could suspend accounts

**Best Practice**: Only refer real, separate people who will use their own devices.

---

**No Limit**: Refer as many as you want.

**Yes, with Guidelines**:

### "What happens if my referral violates terms?"

**Scenarios**:

**Your Responsibility**: 
- Don't knowingly refer people who will cheat
- Don't help people game the system
