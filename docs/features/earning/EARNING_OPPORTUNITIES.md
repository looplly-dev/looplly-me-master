# Earning Opportunities Guide

## Overview

Looplly offers multiple ways to earn money by sharing your opinions, watching content, completing tasks, and contributing your data. This guide covers all earning methods, typical payouts, requirements, and strategies for maximizing your income.

---

## All Earning Methods

### 1. Surveys üìù

**What It Is**: Share your opinions on products, services, brands, and topics through structured questionnaires.

#### Earning Potential
- **Typical Payout**: $0.50 - $5.00 per survey
- **Duration**: 5-20 minutes
- **Frequency**: 3-15 surveys per week (varies by profile)
- **Monthly Potential**: $20-$150+

#### Requirements
- ‚úÖ Complete profile (at least Level 1)
- ‚úÖ Age 18+ (or legal age in your country)
- ‚úÖ Verified mobile number
- ‚úÖ Answer honestly and consistently

#### How It Works

1. **Survey Matching**
   - AI matches you based on profile data
   - Demographics, interests, and behaviors
   - Better profile = more matches

2. **Survey Invitation**
   - Appears in Earn tab when available
   - Shows estimated payout and duration
   - Limited spots (first come, first served)

3. **Qualification Check**
   - First 2-3 questions verify eligibility
   - If you don't qualify, survey ends (no payment)
   - Qualification rate improves with complete profile

4. **Complete Survey**
   - Answer all questions honestly
   - Don't rush (quality matters)
   - Consistent answers improve future matches

5. **Earn Reward**
   - Payment added to Pending Balance
   - AI verification: 24-48 hours
   - Moves to Available Balance when verified

#### Payout Examples

| Survey Type | Duration | Typical Payout |
|------------|----------|----------------|
| **Quick Poll** | 2-3 min | $0.25 - $0.50 |
| **Standard Survey** | 8-12 min | $1.00 - $2.00 |
| **In-Depth Survey** | 15-20 min | $2.50 - $5.00 |
| **Premium Survey** | 20-30 min | $5.00 - $10.00 |

#### Tips for More Surveys

‚úÖ **Complete Your Profile**
- Level 1 (Essential): 3-5 surveys/week
- Level 2 (Standard): 8-12 surveys/week
- Level 3 (Premium): 12-20+ surveys/week

‚úÖ **Answer Consistently**
- Don't contradict previous answers
- AI tracks consistency across surveys
- Inconsistent = lower quality score = fewer invites

‚úÖ **Check Regularly**
- New surveys posted throughout the day
- Popular surveys fill up quickly
- Check 2-3 times daily for best results

‚úÖ **Maintain High Completion Rate**
- Finish surveys you start
- Don't abandon mid-survey (hurts your score)
- Quality matters more than speed

- **Use Case**: Platform improvement
- **Control**: Automatic if opted in

#### How to Enable

1. Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Privacy & Data Sharing**
2. Toggle on desired data types
3. Review what's shared and privacy protections
4. Confirm consent
5. Start earning passively

#### Privacy & Security

‚úÖ **What We DO**:

‚ùå **What We DON'T Share**:

**What It Is**: Earn small bonuses simply for opening the app daily.

#### Earning Potential
- **Payout**: $0.05 - $0.20 per day
- **Bonus**: Multiplies with streaks
- **Monthly Potential**: $3-$12

#### How It Works

3. **Earn Bonus**

4. **Miss a Day**
   - Streak resets to 0
   - Start building again from day 1
   - Bonus returns to base rate

**Pro Tip**: Set daily reminder to maintain streaks!

## Common Questions

### "How much can I realistically earn per month?"

**Realistic Ranges by Effort Level**:

- **Casual** (30 min/day, Level 1 profile): $20-$40/month
- **Regular** (1 hour/day, Level 2 profile): $60-$120/month
- **Dedicated** (2 hours/day, Level 3 profile): $150-$300/month
- **Plus Referrals**: Add $5-$50+/month

**Note**: Varies by region, profile quality, and opportunity availability.

---

### "Why don't I see many surveys?"

**Common Reasons**:
1. ‚ùå Incomplete profile (most common)
2. ‚ùå Low quality score from past surveys
3. ‚ùå Demographics not in demand currently
4. ‚ùå Peak competition times (everyone checking at once)

**Solutions**:
- Complete your profile to Level 2 or 3
- Maintain high completion rates
- Check at different times of day
- Be patient (invites vary week to week)

---

### "Can I do this full-time?"

**Realistic Perspective**:
- Looplly is designed for **supplemental income**, not full-time
- Best for students, side-hustlers, stay-at-home parents
- Treats it as "spare time" money, not primary income
- Full-time earnings rarely exceed $300-$500/month

**Better Approach**:
- Combine with other gig work
- Use during downtime (commute, breaks, evenings)
- Set realistic expectations

---

### "Do earnings expire?"

**No Expiration** on balances:
- Available Balance: No expiration
- Pending Balance: Moves to Available within 48 hours
- Total Earned: Lifetime tracking

**But Note**:
- Inactive accounts (90+ days) may be suspended
- Log in at least once every 90 days to keep active
