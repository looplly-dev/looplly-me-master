## Common Questions

### "How much can I earn per month?"

**Realistic Ranges**:
- **Casual** (30 min/day): $20-$40/month
- **Regular** (1 hour/day): $60-$120/month
- **Dedicated** (2 hours/day): $150-$300/month

**Depends On**:
- Profile completion level (biggest factor)
- Time invested daily
- Quality score
- Region and demographics
- Luck (survey availability)

**Learn More**: [Realistic Earnings](./EARNING_OPPORTUNITIES.md#how-much-can-i-realistically-earn-per-month)

### "When will I see earnings in my wallet?"

**Timeline**:
1. Complete activity: Immediate
2. Pending Balance: Shown instantly
3. AI verification: 24-48 hours
4. Available Balance: Ready to withdraw

**Learn More**: 
- [Payment Timeline](./EARNING_OPPORTUNITIES.md#payment-timeline)

---

### "Do surveys expire?"

**Survey Invitations**: Yes, fill quickly (30-60 minutes typical)

**Your Earnings**: No, never expire
- Available Balance: No expiration
- Pending Balance: Clears within 48 hours
- Account must stay active (log in every 90 days)

---

### "Can I do this full-time?"

**Realistic Answer**: No, Looplly is designed for **supplemental income**.

**Best Used For**:
- Extra spending money
- Side income for students
- Spare time earnings
- Saving for specific goals

**Not Ideal For**:
- Primary income source
- Full-time work replacement
- Paying rent/bills

**Learn More**: [Can I Do This Full-Time?](./EARNING_OPPORTUNITIES.md#can-i-do-this-full-time)

## Earning Strategies

### Beginner Strategy

**Goal: Earn Your First $10**

Week 1:
1. Complete Level 1 profile (required)
2. Verify mobile number
3. Complete 2-3 surveys
4. Watch 10-15 videos
5. Enable daily check-ins

**Expected**: $3-$5 first week

---

### Intermediate Strategy

**Goal: Earn $50-$80/Month**

Ongoing:
1. Complete Level 2 profile (3x more surveys)
2. Check app 2x daily for new surveys
3. Maintain 80%+ completion rate
4. Complete 2-3 surveys daily
5. Fill gaps with videos and tasks
6. Build 7-day streak for 1.2x multiplier

**Expected**: $50-$80/month with 1 hour daily

---

### Advanced Strategy

**Goal: Earn $120-$200/Month**

Ongoing:
1. Complete Level 3 profile (access premium surveys)
2. Check app 3x daily (morning, lunch, evening)
3. Maintain 90%+ completion rate and quality score
4. Complete 3-5 surveys daily (including premium)
5. Add videos, tasks, data sharing
6. Build 30-day streak for 2.0x multiplier
7. Refer 5-10 friends per month ($1.75-$3.50)

**Expected**: $120-$200/month with 2 hours daily

**Learn More**: [Weekly Earning Strategy](./EARNING_OPPORTUNITIES.md#weekly-earning-strategy)
