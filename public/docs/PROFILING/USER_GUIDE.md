## The 3 Profile Levels

### Level 1: What You Told Us at Signup
**5 Questions (Required)**
- First Name
- Last Name
- Mobile Number
- Email Address
- Home Address (verified via Google Places)

**Why We Ask**: These are essential for account creation, fraud prevention, and location-based surveys.

**When You Answer**: During signup (one-time only)

---

### Level 2: Essential Info to Unlock Earning
**10-15 Questions (Required Before Earning)**
- Gender
- Date of Birth
- Household Income (currency-specific to your country)
- Socioeconomic Classification (SEC/SEM/NCCS depending on country)
- Ethnicity
- Employment Status

**Why We Ask**: These are **core demographic fields** that every survey requires for targeting. Without them, we can't match you with surveys.

**When You Answer**: Before you can access the "Earn" tab

**What Happens If You Don't**: You'll see a banner: _"Complete Your Profile to Start Earning"_ and the Earn tab will be locked.

---

### Level 3: Optional Details for Better Matches
**20-40 Questions (Optional, Contextual)**
- **Employment**: Industry, job title, workplace size, skills
- **Lifestyle**: Hobbies, interests, media consumption, fitness habits
- **Automotive**: Car ownership, brand preference, purchase timeline
- **Technology**: Device ownership, OS preference, software usage
- **Health**: Health insurance, fitness habits, diet
- **Finance**: Banking habits, credit cards, investment experience
- **Shopping**: Brand loyalty, shopping habits, price sensitivity
- **Family**: Marital status, children, pets, household size

**Why We Ask**: These help us match you with **higher-paying, specialized surveys** (e.g., car buyers research, tech product testing).

**When You Answer**: 
- When you click on a specific survey category (e.g., "Before viewing this automotive survey, answer 3 questions about car ownership")
- After completing milestones (e.g., "Congrats on 5 surveys! Answer 5 employment questions to unlock higher-paying opportunities")
- Over time as gentle prompts (e.g., "You've been with us for 7 days! Answer 5 lifestyle questions for better matches")

**Can You Skip**: Yes! Level 3 questions are optional. But skipping means:
- You'll see fewer surveys in that category
- Lower-paying surveys (generic, not specialized)
- More time spent in survey screeners

## Why Profile Data Expires

### Life Changes
Your profile data doesn't stay accurate forever:
- **Income changes**: New job, raise, promotion, job loss
- **Lifestyle changes**: New car, moved house, got married, had kids
- **Shopping habits**: New brands you like, preferences shift
- **Technology**: Upgraded devices, switched phones

### Keeping Survey Matching Accurate
If your data is outdated:
- Surveys won't match your current situation
- You'll screen out more often (wasting time)
- Earnings drop because completion rates are lower

### Advertisers Demand Fresh Data
Survey clients pay 50-70% more for users with data refreshed within the last 90 days. Stale data = lower CPM = lower earnings for you.

## How to Refresh Stale Data

### Quick Refresh (1 Minute Per Question)
1. Navigate to "Profile" tab
2. Look for yellow badges next to questions
3. Click the question â†’ Review your old answer
4. Confirm (if still accurate) or edit (if changed)
5. Click "Save"

**Tip**: Your old answer is pre-filled, so if nothing changed, just click "Confirm" and you're done.

---

### Batch Refresh (If Multiple Questions Are Stale)
1. You'll see a banner: _"3 questions need updating"_
2. Click "Update Now"
3. All stale questions appear in one form
4. Review and update each answer
5. Click "Save All"

**Time**: ~3-5 minutes for 3-5 questions

## What Happens If You Don't Update?

### Level 2 Stale (Gender, DOB, Income, SEC)
**Impact**: **Earning blocked** until you refresh

### Level 3 Stale (Employment, Lifestyle, Automotive, etc.)

**What Happens**:
- You'll see **fewer specialized surveys** (e.g., automotive surveys if car ownership data is stale)

- More time spent in survey screeners

**Action Required**: Optional, but recommended to maximize earnings

## Grace Period

You get **7 days** after data expires before enforcement kicks in:

**Timeline**:
```
Day 180: Income question expires (yellow badge appears)
Day 181-186: Grace period (gentle reminders only)
Day 187: Hard block (earning locked until you update)
```

**Why**: We don't want to lock your account the moment data expires. You have a full week to refresh at your convenience.

## FAQ

**Q: Why do you need my address?**
A: For location-based surveys and fraud prevention. We verify addresses via Google Places API to ensure accuracy.

**Q: Can I skip Level 2 questions?**

- Lifestyle: Every 365 days
- DOB/Gender: Never (immutable)

**Q: What happens if I skip Level 3 questions?**

**Q: Can I change my date of birth or address?**
A: Noâ€”these are immutable after signup for fraud prevention. If you made a mistake, contact support.

**Q: Do I get reputation points for completing my profile?**

**Q: How do I know if my data is stale?**
A: Look for yellow badges on the Profile tab or check the banner on your dashboard.
